# Chinese Character Lists and Downloadable Resources

## Official Government Standards

### Ministry of Education Character Lists

#### 《通用规范汉字表》(General Standard Chinese Characters List, 2013)
- **Total Characters**: 8,105 characters
- **Level 1**: 3,500 characters (most common, for general use)
- **Level 2**: 3,000 characters (for names and specialized use)
- **Level 3**: 1,605 characters (for cultural and historical texts)
- **Access**: Available through National Language Commission website
- **URL**: http://www.moe.gov.cn/jyb_sjzl/ziliao/

#### Primary School Character Requirements (小学识字要求)
**Grade 1**: 
- Recognition: 400 characters
- Writing: 100 characters
- Key characters: 一人口手足日月水火山石田土

**Grade 2**:
- Recognition: 800 characters (cumulative)
- Writing: 300 characters (cumulative)
- Added categories: Family members, basic verbs, numbers to 100

**Grade 3-6 Progressive Lists**:
- Available in curriculum standard documents
- Organized by semantic radicals and frequency

## Sample Character Progressions

### Early Elementary (Grades 1-2)
**Visual/Pictographic Characters**:
人 (person), 大 (big), 小 (small), 上 (up), 下 (down)
山 (mountain), 水 (water), 火 (fire), 土 (earth), 木 (wood)

**Numbers and Quantities**:
一二三四五六七八九十 (1-10)
多 (many), 少 (few), 个 (classifier)

**Basic Actions**:
看 (look), 听 (listen), 说 (speak), 走 (walk), 跑 (run)

### Mid Elementary (Grades 3-4)
**Family and Relationships**:
爸爸 (father), 妈妈 (mother), 哥哥 (older brother), 姐姐 (older sister)
朋友 (friend), 老师 (teacher), 学生 (student)

**School and Learning**:
学校 (school), 书 (book), 笔 (pen), 纸 (paper), 课 (class)
学习 (study), 考试 (exam), 作业 (homework)

**Time and Dates**:
年 (year), 月 (month), 日 (day), 星期 (week)
今天 (today), 明天 (tomorrow), 昨天 (yesterday)

### Advanced Elementary (Grades 5-6)
**Abstract Concepts**:
想 (think), 觉得 (feel/think), 希望 (hope), 相信 (believe)
重要 (important), 困难 (difficult), 容易 (easy)

**Complex Compound Characters**:
Characters with multiple components and meanings
Integration of classical Chinese elements
Cultural and literary vocabulary

## Data Format Specifications

### Standard File Formats
- **Text Files**: UTF-8 encoded .txt files
- **Spreadsheets**: .xlsx format with multiple sheets
- **Database**: SQLite or CSV for structured data
- **JSON**: For web applications and APIs

### Character Data Structure
```json
{
  "character": "学",
  "pinyin": "xué",
  "english": "study, learn",
  "strokes": 8,
  "radical": "子",
  "components": ["子", "冖"],
  "frequency_rank": 47,
  "hsk_level": 1,
  "grade_level": 2,
  "usage_examples": ["学习", "学校", "学生"]
}
```

### List Organization Standards
- **By Frequency**: Most common characters first
- **By Stroke Count**: Simple to complex progression
- **By Radical**: Grouped by shared components
- **By Theme**: Organized around topics
- **By Grade Level**: Following educational progressions
